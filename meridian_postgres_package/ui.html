Getting Started
Welcome to Meridian
A private concierge exchange for discreet executive introductions. Follow the five phases below — identities remain masked throughout.
1
You're Authenticated as Mike S
Your principal is locked in. Other members see only your alias and masked GMID — your real name is never displayed on the network.
Principal · Active Session · Encrypted
2
Submit a Confidential Brief
Describe the mandate in the Confidential Brief field — operating scope, sector, experience required. Be specific. The concierge engine scores candidates against your language.
Left Panel → Confidential Brief → Consult Concierge
3
Review & Score Candidates
The right panel surfaces up to 10 ranked candidates, each shown by alias and masked GMID only. Use ◎ Reveal Identity on a card to temporarily unmask — it re-masks automatically after 15 seconds.
Right Panel → Candidate Cards
4
Send an Introduction
Click ◈ Send Introduction on the candidate you wish to reach. A private ping is dispatched to their inbox — only your brief and match score are transmitted, never your identity.
Candidate Card → Send Introduction
5
Recipient Reviews & Opens Channel
Click Open My Inbox to open the recipient view in a new tab. They review the introduction via the Connection Request modal, then accept or decline. On acceptance a secure channel opens for both parties.
Open My Inbox → Review → Accept → Chat
